# Contributing to Regrade

**Regrade is proprietary software owned by Preston Jay Susanto.**

This repository is not open source. Do not fork, copy, or redistribute
any part of it without written permission from the owner.

## Before you contribute

1. **Sign required agreements** — Contributors (including unpaid interns and
   volunteers) must complete and sign all agreements provided by Preston Jay
   Susanto *before* receiving repository access. These agreements are handled
   outside this repository.

2. **Get explicit approval** — Do not submit code, designs, or documentation
   unless you have been invited to contribute and assigned a specific task.

3. **No independent use** — You may not use Regrade code, designs, prompts,
   architecture, or business concepts for personal projects, competing products,
   or any purpose outside your assigned contribution work.

  (including all copyrights, patent rights, and trade secret rights) to
  Preston Jay Susanto.

- You waive any moral rights you may have in your contributions to the extent
  permitted by applicable law.

- You represent that your contributions are your original work (or you have
  rights to submit them) and do not violate any third party's rights.

## Pull requests

- Open pull requests only against branches you are assigned to work on.
- All pull requests require review and approval by Preston Jay Susanto (or a
  delegate explicitly authorized in writing) before merge.
- Do not merge your own pull requests unless explicitly authorized.
