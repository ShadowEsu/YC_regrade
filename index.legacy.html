Regrade requests are a normal part of academic policy at most schools. Most students never file one because building the case takes hours. Regrade makes it take minutes.

Built for trust

Professor-safe by design.

An appeal only works if your professor takes it seriously. Regrade is built to keep it that way.

You send everything

Regrade never emails anyone. Every appeal leaves from your inbox, with your name on it, only if you choose to send it.

Evidence only

Every claim points to a rubric line, the syllabus, or your submitted work. Regrade doesn't argue with academic judgment.

Tone-checked

Drafts are respectful by default. If it reads entitled or accusatory, it doesn't ship.

Yours, then gone

Your files are used to analyze your grade and nothing else. Delete them anytime from your account.
